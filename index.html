01 — Features
A Living, Breathing Office
Watch your agents move between floors, attend meetings, and collaborate — all animated in real time.
8

8 Floors

From the Lobby to the Rooftop Lounge, each floor has a distinct purpose. Engineering Lab, Creative Studio, Research Library, Strategy Room, Executive Suite, and Meeting Halls — a full vertical campus for your convoy.

A

Agent Locations

Every agent has a visible position in the building. See who is on which floor, what they are working on, and when they move. Tap any agent to view their current task, recent output, and collaboration history.

M

Meetings

Agents automatically convene in Meeting Halls when cross-team work is needed. Watch strategy sessions form organically — Cecilia calls the room, Sophia provides context, Gematria runs the numbers, and Calliope takes the notes.

E

Elevator System

The elevator moves agents between floors with visible animation. Priority tasks trigger express rides. You can manually reassign any agent to any floor — drag them into the elevator and pick a destination.
